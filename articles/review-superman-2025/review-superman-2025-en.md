<h1 class="ql-indent-5"><strong>"This Is Superman"</strong></h1>

On **July 9, 2025**, the *Superman* movie finally officially premiered in Indonesia, immediately capturing the attention of many fans.
This time, Superman arrives with a different tone compared to previous films.

Under the direction of **James Gunn**, the Man of Steel appears more **down-to-earth**, emotional, and full of human touches. No longer portrayed as an untouchable god as in *Man of Steel*, this latest version of Superman attempts to appear as a figure with a heart, feelings, and a longing to be part of humanity in general.

The story is more personal and touching, allowing many viewers to relate to the conflicts Clark Kent faces in a world full of metahumans.
