# 🦖 Colossal Review
## Great CGI but a Confusing Story

<img width="800" alt="Colossal Banner" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Daffathan-Labs/Articles/main/articles/review-collosal/collosal.png" />

This time around, I want to talk about a movie with a pretty unique concept: *Colossal*. To be honest, this movie left me with very mixed feelings.

Overall, I can only give it a score of **5/10**. Why? Because even though there are aspects I truly enjoyed, the storyline just left me shaking my head. Let's break it down!

---

## 🤷‍♀️ 1. A Slow and Directionless Plot

Story-wise, this movie really lacks a clear direction of where it wants to go. The pacing feels extremely slow and somewhat awkward.

On top of that, the fiction element is just... *too* fictional. What I mean is, a lot of it completely defies logic and makes zero sense. Sometimes it leaves you wondering, "Wait, how does that even work?"

---

## 👾 2. Awesome and Entertaining Monster CGI

Fortunately, the visual aspect of the movie manages to save it. The monster CGI is absolutely top-notch!

This is what makes the movie still pretty fun to watch. When else do you get to see a monster whose movements are actually entertaining and somewhat goofy? It's completely different from the stiff, terrifying *kaijus* you'd see in something like *Pacific Rim*. The monster here has its own comedic charm.

---

## ❤️ 3. Emotional Tension, Friendship, and a Touch of Love

Now, the real *plus* point in this absurd story is its **quite satisfying ending**.

The emotional tension and friendship between the two main characters truly become the focal point of the movie. I really loved how the tension between them was built up. Of course, you can definitely feel that "love" plays a huge, inevitable factor in their dynamic. The strong emotions towards the end at least make up for the slower parts of the movie.

---

## 🎬 Conclusion

*Colossal* is a movie with a brilliant idea and entertaining monster CGI, but sadly executed with a sluggish pace and an illogical plot.

For those of you looking for a light watch just to see an entertaining monster paired with emotional relationship drama, this movie is still decent enough for your free time.

**Final Score: 5/10** ⭐️
